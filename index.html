How We Measure

Methodology

Every company in the index is scored using the same three-step process — no manual curation, no exceptions.

01
Query Generation

For each company we generate the questions a real buyer would ask — simulating purchase research across AI assistants.

02
Multi-Engine Testing

Queries are submitted to ChatGPT and Perplexity — the two AI systems most used for brand research and vendor discovery.

03
Accuracy Scoring

Responses are evaluated across four dimensions: category accuracy, explanation quality, context relevance, and feature recognition. Scores run 0–100.
